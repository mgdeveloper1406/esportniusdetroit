Ingredients


For main dish

1 cup Greek yogurt

1/2 cup granola (low sugar)

1/2 cup mixed berries

1 tbsp honey


Directions

Step 1
In a glass, layer Greek yogurt, granola, and mixed berries.


Step 2
Drizzle with honey on top


Step 3
Serve and enjoy!
